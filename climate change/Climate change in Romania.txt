The Ministry of the Environment and Climate Change (Romanian: Ministerul Mediului și Schimbărilor climatice) is one of the ministries of the Government of Romania. The institution was known as the Ministry of Environment and Waters Management (Romanian: Ministerul Mediului și Gospodăririi Apelor) before April 2007; and then the Ministry of Environment and Forests.
