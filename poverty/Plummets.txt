Moyie Falls el. 1,936 feet (590 m), Boundary County, Idaho, is an 85 foot (26 m) waterfall on the Moyie River  Crashing through a rocky canyon, the Moyie River descends in tiered form. While the upper portion plummets 60 to 100 feet under an obsolete bridge connecting the gorge, the lower portion tumbles 20 to 40 feet.
